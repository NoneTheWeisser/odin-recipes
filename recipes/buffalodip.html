Buffalo Chicken Dip

Description

I mean what do we really need to say about this dip. It's a creamy, spicy, salty work of art.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 8 ounces cream cheese, softened
  • 4 ounces crumbled blue cheese
  • 1/2 cup buffalo sauce, divided
  • 1/2 cup ranch dressing
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups cooked shredded chicken

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. In large bowl combine cream cheese, sour cream & ranch dressing.
  3. Toss chicken with 1/2 cup buffalo sauce.
  4. Mix in shredded cheddar cheese and crumbled blue cheese.
  5. Spread into the bottom of (8 or 9-inch) deep-dish or small casserole dish.
  6. Bake 15 minutes or until cooked through.

Optional - Slowcooker

  • Add to slowcooker and cook on high for 2 hours or low for 4 hours.
